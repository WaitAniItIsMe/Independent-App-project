Physics Mod

This mod adds REAL LIFE PHYSICS TO THE GAME!! So now if u chop down a tree just be aware for that tree falling on your head.. This mod also helps to turn minecraft into an epic real world simulation.

Guns Galore

Guns Galore Mod 1.16.5 introduces into the game realistic guns like the AK-47, the AR-15 and RPG as well as specialized weapon like a sniper rifle. These weapons are often unfit in environment ssuch as Minecraft, yet adding them will make the game a lot more fun as they facilitate the campaign to conquer the overworld. Me and my friends use this mod and it is really fun to use in our server!

Alex's Mob

Alexs Mob Mod 1.16.5 implements into the game a plethora of creatures which constitutes rare in real life. IN simple words this mod helps to add rare and exquisite animals in ur minecraft world. Next time u go in a river, just be careful CUZ THERE MIGHT BE A HUNGRY CORCODILE WAITING TO EAT YOU!!

Extra Golems Mod

Extra Golems Mod 1.16.5 adds in a large number of new golems that you can create! They have cool abilities and all have there own unique look and power.It has 35+ golems made from vanilla blocks, ranging from wood and glass to diamond and nether brick!

Good Night’s Sleep Mod

Good Night’s Sleep Mod 1.16.5 adds two new dimensions, the good dream, and the nightmare. Each dreamland is accessed through sleeping in its corresponding bed, rather than stepping through a portal. This. Is. Epic.
